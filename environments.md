# Dota 2 World Locations

A comprehensive guide to locations in the Dota 2 universe, extracted from the Dota 2 Wiki.

---

## Abysm

The Abysm is a hellish dimension in the Dota 2 universe described as a realm of endless depths filled with "astral rocks." It serves as the home of the character Nevermore, who harvests souls that swirl in and out of this mysterious plane. The being also refers to this location by an alternative name: "The Rift."

**Associated with:**
- Shadow Fiend (Nevermore) - The primary inhabitant and master of the Abysm; harvests souls within this realm
- Seven Hells - Related location

---

## Acid Jungles

The Acid Jungles is a dangerous biosphere located on Jidi Isle across the Bay of Fradj. This large jungle is home to highly venomous creatures that have evolved to thrive in its toxic environment. The region is notable for transforming the herbalist Lesale into the Venomancer after he was stung by a poisonous reptile while seeking rare plants.

**Associated with:**
- Venomancer - Lesale, a herbalist, ventured into the jungles seeking herbal essences and was stung by a poisonous reptile, transforming him after seventeen years

---

## Ash Archipelago

The Ash Archipelago is a tropical island chain that serves as a popular retirement destination, particularly for the Keen people. It is historically significant as the location where Aurel, a retired soldier, developed and tested his Gyrocopter invention.

**Associated with:**
- Gyrocopter (Aurel) - A former soldier who retired here and developed his Gyrocopter

---

## Ashkavor

Ashkavor is a land near the mountains that lead to Fellstrath, home to the hero Grimstroke and his former lover Yaovhi. The region is renowned for its Ascended Ones, specially chosen individuals who undergo rituals to become guardians. However, the civilization was devastated when Grimstroke corrupted the ascension ritual, transforming all his kin into cursed shades in what became known as the Ink Tide.

**Associated with:**
- Grimstroke - Hero from this region
- Yaovhi - Grimstroke's former lover
- The Ascended Ones - Guardian order of Ashkavor
- Fellstrath - Connected via nearby mountains

---

## Astral Plane

The Astral Plane is one of The Seven Planes in Dota 2's cosmology. It serves as a source of enchanted alloys used for crafting weapons and is accessible to certain heroes through their abilities.

**Associated with:**
- Elder Titan - Able to call his Astral Spirit from the Astral Plane
- Morphling - Associated with this location
- Void Spirit - Can create tears in the Astral Plane, allowing him to traverse through and damage everything in his path

---

## Augury Bay

Augury Bay was a technologically advanced port city, likely inhabited by the Keen race, that was destroyed by Treant Protectors. The city's primary survivor was Rizzrack, who later became known as Timbersaw. The city possessed walls and gates, but these defenses were not sufficient in repelling the Protectors.

**Associated with:**
- Timbersaw (Rizzrack) - Survivor of the city's destruction
- Treant Protector - Force responsible for attacking and destroying the city

---

## Auren

Auren is a river that flows through the Coldbank Plains region, eventually leading toward Goldlake. The river appears in the lore of the Dota 2 universe as part of the world's geography.

**Associated with:**
- Coldbank Plains - The region the river flows through
- Goldlake - The destination toward which the river flows

---

## Aziyog

Aziyog is an underground city constructed from obsidian, situated beneath the magma layer below volcanoes. It features an enormous subterranean cavern, adorned with fine stonework with walls containing small grottoes. The Abyssal Horde inhabits this realm under their leader's command, seeking expansion to the surface world.

**Associated with:**
- Underlord (Vrogros) - The ruler of Aziyog who leads the Abyssal Horde's aspirations for conquest

---

## Azura

**Associated with:**

- Cobalt - Nearby or related location

## Bay of Fradj

The Bay of Fradj is a body of water adjacent to the Jidi Isle, notable for its calm waters. The herbalist Lesale was able to cross it on a coracle, a small boat. The Fradjan Moth species takes its name from this bay.

**Associated with:**
- Venomancer - Connected through the hero's lore
- Lesale - The herbalist who traversed the bay's waters

---

## Bleeding Hills

The Bleeding Hills is a highland region adjacent to The Hoven, characterized by black tar flowing downward into pools below. This terrain serves as the homeland of hill trolls and is notable for its harsh, otherworldly geography within the Dota 2 universe.

**Associated with:**
- Shadow Shaman (Rhasta) - Born in the Bleeding Hills
- Clinkz - Associated with the region
- Troll Warlord - Connected to this troll-inhabited area
- Hill trolls - Native inhabitants
- Hellwether rams - Native fauna

---

## Blueheart Glacier

The Blueheart Glacier is a frozen river flowing through Icewrack, originating in The Barrier and emptying into the Boiling Sea. It serves as a conduit for all frost magic in the world and possesses its own consciousness that can manifest as an avatar to teach worthy mortals.

**Associated with:**
- Crystal Maiden (Rylai) - Sent to Icewrack by her parents, trained under the Ice Wizard at the glacier's crown
- Tusk - Connected to this region
- Ice Wizard - Rylai's mentor who descended into the glacier to hibernate
- Frostiron Tribe - Value the sapphires found within the glacier

---

## Boiling Sea

The Boiling Sea is a northern region where the Blueheart Glacier flows into the Boiling Sea, forming a land of steam and slush. Located near Icewrack and The Barrier, it may be identical to the Northern Sea.

**Associated with:**
- Crystal Maiden (Rylai) - Sent here by the Ice Wizard, spent months in meditation and discovered her spell Snowdrop
- Ice Wizard - Rylai's master who directed her here

---

## Bone Ruins

The Bone Ruins is an excavation site featuring a damaged triumphal arch at its entrance. Meepo owns the keys that open the arch and has likely cleared out bandits from the ruins. A large bird skull discovered at the site is now worn by Meepo as shoulder armor.

**Associated with:**
- Meepo - Owns the keys to the arch and actively explores the excavation site

---

## Bonemeal Ridge

Bonemeal Ridge is a location in the Dota 2 world that serves as the domain of the character Bogdugg and his faction. It is the domain of Bogdugg and the Bonemeal Ogres.

**Associated with:**
- Bogdugg - Primary character associated with this location
- Bonemeal Ogres - The faction inhabiting this area
- Ogres - The race that populates the region

## Candoness

Candoness is a kingdom in the Dota 2 world ruled by King Tansis XIII. The realm was historically threatened by the dragon Uldorak, who demanded tribute upon each royal coronation until the Dragon Knight eliminated the creature.

**Associated with:**
- Dragon Knight (Sir Davion) - Slew Uldorak during Tansis XIII's coronation
- Tansis XIII - Current king
- Tansis XII - Deceased father
- Dowager Queen Tansis XII - Mother
- Uldorak - Fearsome dragon, now deceased

## Cathedral of Rumusque

The Cathedral of Rumusque is a religious institution inhabited by clergy known as "The Rumusque Faithful." These clergymen oppose death, decay, and corruption, spreading holy teachings and promises of spiritual salvation. The cathedral was devastated by plague, killing most of its priests, though a few survivors like Rotund'jere (who became Necrophos) endured.

**Associated with:**
- Necrophos - Survivor of the cathedral's plague ward who transformed after exposure to ensorcelled spells
- Pudge - Connected to the Fields of Endless Carnage, which the cathedral's clerics attempted to purge
- Lady Anshu - High Priestess
- Crella - Grand priestess who forged the Chains of Abscession
- Abajen - Associated character

---

## Claszureme

Claszureme is a realm existing outside of time, named after the god Clasz and characterized by infinite halls. Entry requires deep understanding of Clasz's teachings, and the space between it and other realms is described as extremely cold.

**Associated with:**
- Faceless Void (Darkterror) - Originates from Claszureme, can traverse the realm by tearing holes in time
- Puck - Has visited Claszureme and learned about its inhabitants
- Dhreg Cult - Likely based in Claszureme with chapels throughout the realm
- Clasz - God who rules over Claszureme

---

## Dark Reef

Dark Reef is an underwater prison housing dangerous sea creatures and criminals. Built within a deep ocean trench and covered by a metallic grid, the facility features a dark labyrinth interior patrolled by eels and guarded by giant stinging anemones. Explosive mines are chained floating near the exterior, where several ships have wrecked.

**Associated with:**
- Slark - The only prisoner ever to successfully escape, exploiting a distraction created by the Dark Reef Dozen's failed escape attempt
- Slithereen, Deep Ones, and Meranth - Prisoners
- Siltbreaker - Mysterious character
- Skadi - Goddess with some connection to the prison

---

## Foulfell

Foulfell is a prison planea hidden dimension where demons imprison their outlaws. Described as a dark mirror of reality, lined with fractal walls that reflect the images of the imprisoned, it may be one of The Seven Planes and is sometimes called the Hell of Hells.

**Associated with:**
- Terrorblade - Imprisoned here after stealing from the Demon Lords; learned to manipulate his reflections and eventually shattered the prison's walls to escape

---

## Ghastly Eyrie

The Ghastly Eyrie serves as the capital city of the Skywrath race. Its location is high above ground, making it difficult for other races to reach. This elevated positioning provides the Skywrath with natural defensive advantages.

**Associated with:**
- Skywrath Mage - Hero from this location
- Vengeful Spirit - Hero associated with this location
- Skywrath - Race that inhabits the city
- Scree'auk - Deity
- Avilliva - Deity

## Hidden Temple

The Hidden Temple is a secret sanctuary discovered by Lanaya through her quest for knowledge. It serves as the residence of the Hidden Onesmystical entities who guard encrypted codices and forbidden secrets. The temple exists in nature itself, accessible only to those the Hidden Ones permit to find it.

**Associated with:**
- Templar Assassin (Lanaya) - Made a vow to serve the Hidden Ones as their assassin
- Void Spirit (Inai) - The master of the temple and most hidden of the Hidden Ones
- Spectre - Associated with this location
- Faerie Dragons - Known to visit the temple
- The Hidden Ones - Deities/entities who reside there

## Icewrack

Icewrack is a frozen northern region sometimes called the Frozen Waste, located south of The Barrier and bordering the Northern Sea or Boiling Sea. The Blueheart Glacier flows through this icy landscape, which is home to the Frostiron Tribe and serves as a place of magical study and refuge.

**Associated with:**
- Crystal Maiden (Rylai) - Sent to Icewrack where she became a student of the Ice Wizard
- Tusk - Native to the area; returns every winter to visit the Frostiron Tribe
- Winter Wyvern (Auroth) - Dragon who chose an abandoned keep in Icewrack as her home
- Ice Wizard - Rylai's mentor
- Great white wolf spirit - Once inhabited or near Icewrack

---

## Narrow Maze

The Narrow Maze is a complex system of twisted passageways that newly deceased souls must navigate before reaching the Underscape. It functions as a sorting mechanism based on intelligence, cunning, and persistence, with souls exiting to either points of light or dark pits. The maze's walls are lined with sharpened blades.

**Associated with:**
- Razor - The Lightning Revenant who serves as the primary guardian, using an electric whip to direct souls
- Visage - Associated through their Familiar, which acts as a guardian of the maze
- Monkey King (Sun Wukong) - Has escaped the maze and erased his name from the Book of the Dead
- The Underscape - Connected location where souls ultimately travel

---

## Nightsilver Woods

The Nightsilver Woods is the sacred forest of Selemene, the Moon Goddess. The Dark Moon Order dedicates itself to protecting this sanctuary, turning away or striking down trespassers with lunar energy. The forest holds significant religious and mythological importance in Dota 2's lore.

**Associated with:**
- Mirana - Princess who guards luminescent sacred lotuses in the forest's silvery pools
- Luna - Connected to the woods; her mount Nova inhabits the forest
- Underlord (Vrogros) - Threatened to invade and burn the Nightsilver Woods
- Monkey King (Sun Wukong) - Occasionally spends summers in the forest
- Nova - Creature serving as Luna's mount
- Lunar Moth and Felmoth - Sacred beings believed to inhabit the forest

---

## Seven Hells

The Seven Hells are demon realms of eternal torment and darkness in Dota 2's world. According to Terrorblade, "hell is a sieve" while heaven is exclusive, suggesting hell readily accepts sinful souls. Between each hell layer exists a membrane that only Doom can freely cross.

**Associated with:**
- Doom - The supreme Demon Lord and sovereign ruler of all seven hells
- Shadow Demon - Second-ranking Demon Lord with dominion over parts of the hells
- Shadow Fiend - Least of the Demon Lords; seeks to usurp Doom's seventh hell dominion
- Terrorblade - Criminal demon imprisoned in Foulfell after breaking infernal laws
- Clinkz - Slew Maraxiform of the Sixth Hell; eternally burned by its fire
- Legion Commander - Corrupted warrior fighting for all seven hells
- Lion - Descended into hell for revenge; mastered demonic sorcery
- Muerta - Psychopomp who brings the dead to the seven hells
- Undying, Visage, Wraith King - Various relationships to the infernal realms

## The Seven Planes

The Dota 2 universe is structured into seven planes of existence, each with distinct characteristics. Physical beings traveling between these planes experience dangerous friction damage, while spirits lost between worlds eventually become minerals. Traversing the dimensional boundaries presents significant hazards, including encounters with interdimensional predators.

**Associated with:**
- Elder Titan - Associated with the Astral Plane and the Primordial Plane
- Spectre - Connected to an unnamed plane containing chaotic emanations and violent power
- Spirit Breaker - Originates from either the Elemental Plane or an unnamed plane that casts the Terrene Plane as its shadow

## Ultimyr Academy

The Ultimyr Academy is an institution devoted to teaching arcane magic and magical studies. Its most renowned student, Demnok Lannik, attended to learn spells that would aid his pursuit of discovering ancient texts. The academy employs scribes who translate old magical runes.

**Associated with:**
- Invoker - Connected through Demnok Lannik, the academy's most celebrated pupil
- Warlock - Also listed as associated with this magical institution

## The Barrier

The Barrier is a series of mountains located in the extreme north. It is an icy landscape of peaks and glaciers, preceded by frozen tundras and Icewrack to the south. Nearby settlements include Cobalt, White Fields, and Wolfsden Tavern.

**Associated with:**
- Tusk - Primary hero connected to this location; resides in the Barrier region
- Icewrack wolves - Inhabit the area near the mountains

## The Barrens

The Barrens is a vast wasteland, once ruled by a king. This desolate region may be situated near or within the Scintillant Waste and was formerly inhabited by the Spire Mage.

**Associated with:**
- Sand King - Hero linked to this location
- Spire Mage - Notable figure who once dwelled in the Barrens

## The Hoven

The Hoven is a large forest located at the base of the Bleeding Hills spanning approximately one thousand leagues. The woodland contains pools of black tar flowing from the hills, and ancient trees whose wood crafts shields and armor. It is ruled by the benevolent king-mage Sutherex.

**Associated with:**
- Clinkz - A sworn protector who battled the demon Maraxiform and now patrols as an eternally burning skeleton
- Troll Warlord (Jah'rakal) - Originally from the Hoven but now travels the world
- Sutherex - The benevolent king-mage ruler
- Maraxiform - Demon from the sixth hell defeated by Clinkz

---

## The Outworld

The Outworld is a mysterious crystalline realm positioned at the edge of the Void, serving as the homeworld of Harbinger's race. Described as "The World on the Rim of the Abyss," this place is characterized by its unique jagged crystalline structures and abundant arcane energy.

**Associated with:**
- Outworld Destroyer (Harbinger) - The sole known inhabitant who maintains eternal vigilance over this realm

## The Void

**Associated with:**
