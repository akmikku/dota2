* basically intro act where players land on tourist island when offered a free getaway as part of a promotion for a casino. 
* players land on the Jidi Isles in the Tangled Tropics
* Explore / interact with npcs to take quests near the area but the main one quest is offered by the owner of the casino strip - Jin'Zakk (Batrider) 
* Barkeeps and casino guests will offer you to clear up the area and take care of the plague spreading from the north of the isles and offer money
* Before you can truly take out the threat in the north though batrider will test your abilities by sending you on a quest to the howling weald 

* Howling weald is a wartorn location where lucius longclaw (lycan) was fighting against Bogdugg before he went missing 
* Now the ogres have run over the area and there are a few commanders who need your help setting up an outpost and driving the ogres out of the island 
* This is the quest that Batrider sends you on to prove yourself before you can clear up the poision destroying the North (venomancer) 

* After working through fighting against all the enemies in Howling Weald and defeating Bogdugg the party returns back to the Jidi Isles and learns about Venmoancer's story 
* The party then takes the final quest of act 1 to venture into the Acid Jungles of the Northern Jidi Isles and defeat Venomancer while uncovering the secrets hidden within the jungle
* Very loot focused and dungeon-y experience to start off the DND session
