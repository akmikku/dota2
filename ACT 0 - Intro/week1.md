Many affluent, famous or otherwise noteworthy guests have been invited to take part in the festivities rumored to last a month. After exploring the locations and activities the party eventually encounters the big boss of the place - [Batrider](#batrider_info). He explains how he is mentally torn and has two ragin personalities - conservationism and capitalism. Whichever side of him is active acts on things but one thing always remains the same - his love for his beloved pet bats. This has caused him to go to great extents to provide land and sanctuary to his bats but lately a great plague has fallen upon the Jidi Isles. A long time ago a herablist known as [Lesale](#venomancer_info) had gone looking for power in the form of herbs mashed together to create fantastical and potent potions. He stumbled upon a small jungle colony of tribal shamanic folk that lived near the temple of [Aktok](#aktok_info). There he perfected his craft but the power cost him his humanity as he merged flesh and spirit into a posionous reptile with the sole purpose of spreading his noxious plague through the forests of these isles. This poison has been devastating the Morde-bat population and mutating them into glowbats and other types of vicious bat species. 

He needs your party's help and is willing to give you all a substantial sum of money and also connections to future adventures and revenue if you can help him save his bats. He needs you all to get stronger first though because the threat of the Venomancer is not to be taken lightly. His poison seeps through armor and has lingering hallucogenic effects if not removed quickly. A weakly equipped party will stumble and fall in front of the might of Aktok's chosen. This is why he recommends you to visit some of his friends who are trying to unearth an artifact and keep the peace on another island a few nights of travel away - Howling Weald. He tells you that he will equip you with food and rations for travel and needs you to take a scroll that may assist the efforts of his friends in the Howling Weald - Captain Lightfoot & Commander Targan. 

Should you accept the quest you will be offered a hefty sum of 500 gold per person half paid upon arrival from the Howling Weald and the other half paid upon the termination of the Venomancer.

<summary> Batrider / Jin'Zakk </summary> 
Batrider was born in the Rama Yaskav Jungle and spent his youth in and out of captivity. He became talented at taming the Morde Bats that roamed the jungle at night. After seeing the cycle of captivity he decided to try and create this isle of captivity and torture into a place of rest and peace for travelers. His plans were working well and he would be soon be wealthy beyond imagination but unbenknownst to him a plague of evil was spreading in the junlges on the northern isle - Venomancer. </details>

<summary> Aktok </summary>
Little is known about Aktok himself besides being an ancient snake deity who is destined to swallow the world. He has a set of golden decorated armor that's dedicated to him and he has a gilded green eye that bestows vast power to those who wield it. Aktok currently remains dormant until he awakens to fulfill his catastrophic destiny.<br><br>Aktok is worshipped by an obscure cult from an unknown village whose shamans would gather once each season in a certain underground area to offer sacrifices to their god.
